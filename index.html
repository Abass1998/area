Area of a Circle

Workings

Area of a Circle is given below

Area = ( πr²)cm²

Where r stands for radius in (cm) and π is a constant

Area of a Trapezium

Workings

Area of a trapezium is given below

Area = 1/2(a+b)h cm²

Where a, b and h stands for length, breadth, and height in (cm)
